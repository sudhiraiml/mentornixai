About Mentornix AI & Founder

Mentornix AI is dedicated to empowering learners and professionals in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. Our mission is to make advanced tech education accessible, practical, and career-focused for everyone.

Founded by Sudhir Goswami, an AI expert with over a decade of experience, Mentornix AI blends real-world projects, hands-on learning, and mentorship. We believe in teaching not just theory, but the skills and mindset needed to succeed in the fast-evolving tech landscape.

Our courses cover a wide range of topics including Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI, NLP, MLOps, and more. We focus on practical skills, portfolio building, and career guidance to help you land your dream job or advance in your current role.
